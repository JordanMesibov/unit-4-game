Crystal Collector!

Each crystal is assigned a secret value at the beginning of each round! Click on that crystal as many times as you would like in order to recieve points equal to the crystal's value!
The goal is to acquire the same number of points for the round as the target score for the round displayed below!
Be careful, though! If you go over the target round score, you will lose the round!
After you win or lose a round, a new round will start and a new target score will be displayed! The values of the crystals will change, too! BONUS: Try to get 5 wins!
